The dataset used in this project is derived from publicly available sources including the **Census of India (2011)** and literacy-related reports published by **The Times of India**. It contains literacy statistics across Indian states and union territories.

### Key Features of the Dataset:
- State / Union Territory name  
- Overall literacy rate (%)  
- Male literacy rate (%)  
- Female literacy rate (%)  
- Urban literacy rate (%)  
- Rural literacy rate (%)  
- Year of observation (where applicable)
